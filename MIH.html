The Materials Innovation
Hub

Materials Innovation Hub is one of the fastest growing and most rapidly evolving disciplines and one which stands at the forefront of modern technology. When we think of any major challenge we have to face in the near future, Materials are at the center of it. We are in the midst of a Materials revolution and over the next decade, the rapid advancement in materials will have a massive impact. It is of this view that the Materials Innovation Hub is of utmost importance, especially in our part of the world, to drive innovation with Materials as the focus.
