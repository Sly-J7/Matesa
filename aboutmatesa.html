MATESA (mah-tee-sah) is an association under the Ghana Engineering Students' Association (GESA), KNUST-Kumasi.
It comprises students from both Materials Engineering and Metallurgical Engineering.
All alumni from both Materials Engineering and Metallurgical Engineering are deemed eligible to be members of the association.

Aims and Objectives

  • Package, promote, and encourage Materials Engineering and Metallurgical Engineering education through symposiums, Industrial trips, conference, exchange programmes and also to carry out our role in the national and technological development as well as the world of science and inventions.

  • Provision of assistance to both the department and its members when the need arises and also the contribution of our quota to GESA.

  • To provide the students with the platform to obtain practical knowledge concerning the programme.

  • To enhance student-lecturer relationship in order to facilitate the teaching and learning process.

  • Finally to protect the interest of the student body and to serve as the mouth piece for the Students of Materials Engineering and Metallurgical Engineering.
